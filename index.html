About Me

During my time as an electronics technician in the US Navy, I discovered my passion for both troubleshooting technical issues and creating entirely new systemic solutions to keep my equipment up and running. I was the go-to guy for resolving any issues that happen to come upon the shipboard radars.

Today, not much has changed. I still find great reward in creating solutions for any sort of technical issues. The difference is I now also find that this rewarding feeling can be obtained through coding. As a student of devCodeCamp, I’m challenged day in and day out to figure out how I can get from “point A” to “point B”. This process often involves many roadblocks, but when I successfully make it to “point B”, I come out of it with both a wave of excitement as well as coding knowledge I didn’t have before. This has lead to me finding my dream of becoming a full-stack developer.

In my spare time, I am continuously finding ways to continue my quest towards financial independence. This quest involves consistent financial literacy, stock market research, and exploring ways to produce steady passive income. When I’m not exercising my financial goals, you can find me exercising in the gym! I’m a avid believer of keeping up with my health so I can continue to keep a clear head to accomplish my goals.

My Projects

Rock, Paper, Scissor, Lizard, Spock

A console/terminal based game where you, the user, have to option to face another user, or a bot.
The game is play much like the standard "rock, paper, scissors" but two extra gestures are added,
giving you extra variables to take into consideration.

Robots vs. Dinosaurs

Robots vs Dinos is console/terminal based game where teams of Dinos and Robots are pitted against each other. As a user, you hold the power to assign a weapon to the robots as well as choose if you'd like the opposing side to attack back. The game carries out until one team has been completely eliminated.

Most Wanted User Search Engine

This javascript based project provides a database in which you, the user, search for a single individual based off multiple characteristics such as eye color, height, etc. After the person you're searching for is identified, you have the ability to see all of the persons characteristics and family members.

Day Trip Generator

This javascript based project allows the user to randomly generate a day trip that will consist of a destination, a mode of transportation in which you will use to get to your destination, a restaurant you will eat at, and an activity. The user has the choice to change certain parts of the trip as they deem fit, then comfirm the trip when they are happy.
